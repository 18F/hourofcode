How to add your name to the site

  1. Visit the users list page here.
  2. Click on the blue + sign next to the header "hourofcode/_data/users/+"
  3. In the "Name your file..." box, add your username and then ".yml", for exampe: "obama.yml"
  4. Copy this text and then fill in your own info. For the emoji, pick your favorite emoji icon from this list—and be original! 
    name: Barack Obama
agency: The White House
office: Executive Office of the President
emoji: '+1'
  5. Click on the green button "Propose new file"
  6. Click on the green button "Create pull request"
